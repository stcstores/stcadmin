Requires changes in the inventory

Some products may have missing or invalid information that will need to be corrected in the Cloud Commerce inventory.

Requires additional information

FNAC products need to have a FNAC category associated with them and the correct price set. Depending on the product and category, size and colour information may also need to be added.

Translations

All products on FNAC must have their names, descriptions and colour information translated into French.
