Update Offers

Offer files are used to update price and stock levels on FNAC. After a new product is created an offer file must be uploaded before the product will be availble to purchase.

The offer file contains a "Shipping Comment" that describes the expected shipping time and any other relevent shipping information. The shipping comment can be changed below. Enter the new comment (in french) then generate and upload a new offer file.
